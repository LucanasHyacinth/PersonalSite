Korean Love Holidays

1. January 14th: Diary Day/Candle Day

Diaries are given to couples and friends as gifts. Anniversaries, birthdays and other special occasions are recorded into the journals. Also, some celebrate this day as “Candle Day” and give and receive decorative candles.

2. February 14th: Valentine’s Day

Valentine’s Day is celebrated in South Korea like it is everywhere else (in other countries). However there is a slight difference: Only the men get gifts, bummer right? But don’t worry ladies, your frown in going to turn into a smile in March

3. March 14th: White Day

On this day, it’s time for the men to do their part and give us lovely ladies gifts. Some follow the custom that the men should give women gifts that are 3 times the worth of what they received a month ago, ladies rule!

4. April 14th: Black Day

This holiday is mainly for single people. It’s rather a depressing day where single people eat a sticky, black-bean paste noodle dish called jjajangmyun (짜장면) and wish they had a love interest. However, some have turned the depressing day into a celebration where the single people celebrate their independence with noodles!

5. May 14th: Yellow Day/Rose Day

Yellow Day, couples dress in yellow and give each other roses. Singles that didn’t have their fill of lonely-heart noodles on Black Day have the chance to fill up on yellow curry and hope their love life will “spice up.”

6. June 14th: Kiss Day

This is a day to confess your feelings to a crush and start a new relationship with a smooch. Many companies, ranging from lipstick brands to breath mint makers, do their best to capitalize, offering special promotions.

7. July 14th: Silver Day

Couples exchange silver rings and discuss future plans of marriage. Acting as promise rings, the gifts are often given before or after in-person parental introductions.

8. August 14th: Green Day

This is a day designated for drinking soju (a harsh alcohol traditionally made from rice, tasting similar to vodka) and then taking a romantic walk in the woods. Most likely the brain child of a drunk teenager who wanted to be alone with his girlfriend, Green Day is centered around the fact that soju comes in a green bottle.

9. September 14th: Photo Day/Music Day

Most days in Korea are photo and music days, but the 14th of September is when it becomes “official” (or as official as an off-the-calendar holiday can get).

Portrait studios and gimmick photo booths are popular, and it seems like everyone’s taking a reverse-facing camera shot. Noraebangs — singing (karaoke) rooms — are in high demand, as well.

10. October 14th: Wine Day

Couples drink a glass (or bottle) of wine over a romantic dinner. Singles drink away their sorrows.

11. November 14th: Movie Day

Movie theaters are jam-packed on this day, but couples also rent movies, give each other DVD gifts, or go to a DVD room (the “DVD Bang,” or movie room, is a popular Korean hangout on any day). They’re rented for approximately the price of one cinema ticket and can hold a small group of people. The DVDs are somewhat older than what’s playing in the theater, but not by much.

12. December 14th: Hug Day

Running short on reasons to dedicate a special day, Hug Day wraps up the year with a firm and gentle squeeze and a sigh of relief. Couples spend the day hugging each other, while polls on which Korean celebrity is most cuddle-worthy give the singles something to fantasize about. I think this holiday is really adorable!!

Sitting on desks, being strong.. I’m sorry.” He lowered his said and sat down then slowly, his face was burried on the table. I went back to my seat for the last subject which’s Physics. One of my faves because of the teacher, Sir Kim, who was really good at handling classes and never bored me. I was a bit concerned about Jimin though, he was busy texting, probably, Jungkook for missing class.

Most students were cheering and asked us to do one more.

We went back to our seats and then each class went up the stage to take a picture together. And when everyone got their turn, it was time for the last part of prom. The last dance. While my eyes were searching through the crowd for JK, I saw Yoongi holding a drink with Julie and his sister, Yoonji, was talking with Jin and Chesca near the dessert table. I know.. after this, it will be kind of a long time before we can be together again. But I will support you in any way that I can. I promise that.” I finally told him. I felt all the weight in my heart evaporate. I was satisfied and happy with what I’ve decided. Without saying anything else, he said “Thank you..” I smiled and looked at his eyes which were already staring at me. Before I realized we had already stopped dancing and most of the students were seated already.. he kissed me. “Thank you, Y/N.”

The moment I woke up, my phone vibrated. “Everything’s ready! I organized them all already So just take your time and get pretty! Because we are about to receive the award for all of our hard work.” A text from Ariesa. And then I checked my inbox which was full of text messages from my best friends and some were from my sister, Yoonji and the others. An unknown number texted me. It said “Congrats Y/N! I surely will be able to visit your gallery tonight. \Can’t wait to see you and the others. -PJMN” I was glad to receive a text from this person. After all the years we have not seen each other, I was excited to see him again. I heard Jungkook debuted with his best friends in a soon-to-be-LEGENDARY group but I have not heard from him. I was busy travelling to every part of the world anyway. Volunteering to help people and animals of every kind was my hobby. And my job? Painting. I drew every single special memory I ever had from each country. Like taking care of a penguin for 2 months when it was severely wounded. Also, when the team and I camped out and I made a friend which was a fox who loved my tent very much. I named him Vick and although I was a bit scared sometimes, I loved him.

I got ready for the gallery which will be composed of my paintings, Julie’s photographs and Mika’s fashion show with a special guest, Chesca, who was already famous worldwide as an actress and singer. All of us succeeded which made me happy with the thought that our efforts were not put into the trash can. When I arrived at the venue, I found myself looking around and waiting for Jimin and our close friends. The event started but I have not seen them yet. My best friends and I proceeded with the program and greeted the important visitors with admiration and respect. I went to my part of the gallery and saw Hoseok and then Namjoon who was with my sister. I was really happy seeing both of them together again and Hoseok looked amused with the paintings. Then a few minutes later, Mika’s fashion show started and I was seated at the front. Julie was everywhere, trying to take the best pictures. Also in the front seats were Jin, Ariesa and Taehyung. I got up to go nearer to them but a visitor accidentally spilled wine on my dress. She apologized and I hurried to the bathroom. I saw Yoonji standing with a bottle of wine. “You never changed.” I told her and she chuckled a bit. “Here, wear this.” She handed me a paper bag and went out with a smile. I checked what was inside and it was a dress.. the dress I wore for prom. I had no choice, I guess? So I changed and arrived at the show which was ending. I walked to Ariesa and my best friends who were with the guests. They congratulated us for doing such an amazing gallery and gave us great ratings and comments.

There was still one more part of the gallery that I was not able to take a look at– photographs. When I went there minutes ago, it was full of people. But the number has died down a bit now. Julie’s pictures entertained me. Photographs are a bit similar to paintings, you know. The only difference is that you use a camera in one and a brush with the other. I walked and walked.. and met Jimin. He was looking at the photographs and it seemed like he felt all the feelings of that one single photograph. It was a picture of a girl who was looking up the sky from her window and a boy who was looking at her from another window. When he turned around, he looked kind of shocked to see me and then he laughed. “Long time no see.” He pinched my cheeks. We did not talk long but he told me we’d be seeing each other soon after this. He waved goodbye and I continued walking. “Damn, I miss that guy.” I thought to myself as Jimin left. And then I saw one picture that hooked me. The picture..

It was us. Jungkook and I dancing in prom. I went nearer to it. I felt my tears fall down and seconds after, someone hugged me from the back. “Nice dress, princess.” And kissed my cheeks. I turned around. “Kookie..” I whispered. “What was the theme of that prom again?” He asked me with a smile plastered on his face. “One last time.” I remembered and told him. “One last time?” He asked me seriously. “Yes, one last time.” I replied. He held my hands and said “Thank you, Y/N.” Then he moved his hands to my face and kissed me on my forehead.
